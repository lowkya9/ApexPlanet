The Solar System

Our solar system consists of the Sun and the celestial objects bound to it by gravity, including eight planets, moons, asteroids, and comets.

Planets of the Solar System

The eight planets are: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.
